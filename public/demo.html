Two ways to use disk browser in tinyMCE -

  • Click on icon to open tinymce image plugin.
    And then click on link to open disk browser.
    Select an image to be added to the editor.
  • Click on custom button 'Disk browser' to open the disk browser directly.


Two ways to use disk browser in CKEditor -

  • Click on icon to open ck-editor image browser.
    And then click on 'Browser server' link to open disk browser.
    Select an image to be added to the editor.
  • Click on icon to open disk browser.
